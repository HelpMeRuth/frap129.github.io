I compile my own custom toolchains using optimizations for Krait processors to get the bet performance out of any ROM or Kernel. Redesigned Lockscreen Notifications
*Currently a WIP* Liked the custom lockscreen notifications feature available in a lot of kitkat ROMs, but not Lollipop's? Our redesign of the notifications allows them to be hidden with only an icon showing, giving you the same privacy you had before, as well as the less clustered and more sleek feel of the original feature, while incorporating the same material design look Lollipop brings.
